About Amritvan

Rooted in Village Traditions, Crafted for Sacred Everyday Living

Amritvan is a devotional lifestyle brand inspired by the purity of village life, the warmth of Indian traditions, and the spiritual comfort of everyday rituals. Rooted in faith, nature and sacred living, we create products that bring devotion, peace and a sense of grounding into the home.

Our Story

From Village Roots to a Devotional Lifestyle Brand

Amritvan was born in Village Khandwa, Sehore District, Madhya Pradesh — from a life shaped by faith, nature, and the quiet beauty of rural India. In village life, devotion is not reserved for special occasions; it is part of everyday living — in morning prayers, havan rituals, the fragrance of sacred spaces, and the peaceful connection between home, tradition, and the earth.

What began as a small business grew from a simple but meaningful vision: to bring the purity, warmth, and spiritual essence of village life into homes everywhere. Inspired by the values we grew up with, Amritvan was created to offer devotional products that feel authentic, natural, and deeply connected to Indian spiritual living.

For us, Amritvan is more than a brand. It is a way of carrying forward the simplicity of our roots, the devotion of our culture, and the timeless feeling of sacred presence into everyday life.

Our Inspiration

Inspired by the Soul of Indian Village Life

Our inspiration comes from the soul of Indian village life — where nature, tradition, and spirituality exist in harmony. It comes from temple fragrances carried in the air, from the purity of havan and puja rituals, from cow-based sacred traditions, and from homes where devotion is a daily way of life.

We believe devotional products should do more than support rituals — they should elevate the atmosphere around them. They should bring peace, purity, grounding, and a feeling of sacred connection into the home. Every Amritvan product is inspired by this belief and designed to make spiritual moments more meaningful, comforting, and devotional.

Our Mission

Bringing Faith, Fragrance and Tradition into Everyday Homes

Our mission is to create devotional and spiritually inspired products that connect people with the essence of Indian tradition, village values, and sacred living. We aim to offer products that are rooted in purity, crafted with care, and made to enhance prayer, puja, havan, and everyday spiritual practices.

Through Amritvan, we want to preserve the warmth of traditional devotional culture while presenting it in a way that fits modern homes and everyday lifestyles.
